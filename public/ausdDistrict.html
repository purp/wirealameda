Benefits for the district

Availability of Google Fiber in Alameda has the potential to enable many benefits for Alameda Unified School District, in addition to those described for our students and the wider Alameda community. Many of these benefits have common themes of improving efficacy of education delivery, and improving operational efficiency. These are of paramount importance in the current education climate, as the educational needs of our students are changing for the 21st century, and the money available to deliver that education is constantly shrinking.

  • Reducing travel time and cost. Our district includes 10 elementary, 3 middle, and 3 high schools, plus several associated charter schools and an adult school, distributed across 10 square miles. Staff often travel between sites to deliver classes or training. By using online delivery, many trips may be eliminated, saving time and money.

  • Cross-site access to specialized classes. By delivering certain classes online, and making them available across school sites via high-definition video conferencing, the district can simultaneously increase the number of students who have access to the material, and reduce the cost of delivery.

  • Improved staff development. Our teachers and staff continuously refresh their professional skills and knowledge through staff development and training. In professional development, we can leverage many of the same advantages that high-speed internet brings to our students - making use of streamed or recorded high-definition training content to improve our efficiency of delivery, and to provide access to external resources from professional and research institutions, universities, and consultancies.

  • More efficient outsourcing. By radically increasing the bandwidth of our connection to the internet, our ability to interact online and securely share data with external partners is enhanced.

  • Competition. AUSD has its own private network infrastructure, currently operated on a backbone leased from a commercial provider. Availability of Google Fiber will help increase competition in the marketplace of existing providers (AT&T, Comcast, etc.), which should ultimately benefit the District, in terms of more favorable lease terms and enhanced features / capabilities.
